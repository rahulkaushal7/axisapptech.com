Deleting Your Flick Match Account

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Flick Match account and what happens to your data. Flick Match is operated by Axis App LLC.

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

  1. Open Flick Match and go to the Profile tab.
  2. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and personal data are deleted right away.

Option 2 — By email

If you can't access the app, email us at flickmatch@axisapptech.com from the email address on your account, with the subject “Delete my account.” We will verify the request and delete the account within 30 days (usually much sooner).

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login
  • Your profile and country/region setting
  • Your swipes
  • Your followed shows
  • Your selected streaming services
  • Your after-watching feedback
  • Any beta survey responses are kept but de-identified (no longer linked to you)

What is retained, and why

  • Shared matches: matches you created together with a partner remain available to that partner as part of their shared watch history. They are no longer associated with you.
  • We may retain limited information where required to comply with legal obligations or to resolve disputes.
  • Residual copies in backups are purged on a rolling basis, normally within 30 days.

Signing up again

Signing up later with the same email creates a brand-new, empty account; your previous data is not restored.
