🚀 Flagship Product

Flick Match

Choosing what to watch together shouldn't end in a stalemate. Flick Match is a mobile app that lets couples and groups swipe through movies and shows simultaneously — when you both swipe right, it's a match. No more endless scrolling, no more "I don't know, what do you want to watch?"

  • Swipe-based matching for movies and TV shows
  • Real-time sync between partners or group members
  • Smart recommendations based on mutual taste
  • Available on iOS and Android
